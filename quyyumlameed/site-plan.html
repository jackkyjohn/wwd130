Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this personal website is to serve as a dynamic and engaging portfolio that showcases my skills, projects, and expertise as a computer programmer and web developer/designer. Through this website, I aim to provide a comprehensive view of my work, skills, and experiences in the tech industry. It will act as a hub where potential clients, employers, and collaborators can explore my past work, learn about my capabilities, and get in touch with me for future opportunities.

Audience

  • Potential Employers and Clients: The primary audience includes tech companies, startups, and businesses looking to hire a computer programmer or web developer/designer. They can assess my qualifications, see my previous work, and consider me for job opportunities or project contracts.
  • Fellow Developers and Designers: This website will be a valuable resource for the tech community. It will provide insights into my projects, code samples, and design choices, which can be used for reference, learning, or collaboration.
  • Students and Aspiring Developers: Aspiring programmers and web developers/designers can benefit from the educational content, tutorials, and insights shared on the website. They can learn from my experiences and get inspired to pursue a career in tech.
  • Collaborators and Networking: Individuals interested in potential collaboration, open-source contributions, or networking opportunities can use this website to connect with me and discuss shared interests or projects.
